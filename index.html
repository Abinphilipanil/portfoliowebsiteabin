Web-App development

Fitness Tracker App

The Fitness Tracker App is an AI-powered web application built using Streamlit, Pandas, and OpenAI to help users monitor their fitness journey through BMI calculation, workout tracking, and AI-generated fitness recommendations. It allows users to input their details, calculates their BMI, and provides personalized exercise and nutrition suggestions based on their fitness goals. The app features an interactive workout logging system, enabling users to track activities such as running, weightlifting, and yoga. Additionally, it includes an AI-driven Q&A forum, where users can ask fitness-related questions and receive intelligent responses powered by OpenAI’s GPT model. Data persistence is handled through CSV files, ensuring easy retrieval and storage without needing a complex database. The application emphasizes a user-friendly interface, seamless functionality, and data-driven insights to enhance the fitness experience.
